[Why MyONE?]

The MyONE app has a positive impact on the user's mental health in several ways. MyONE provides a friendly, personal support to the user, which aims to increase the user's sense of significance and the feeling of being understood. This can help reduce feelings of loneliness and generally improve well-being.

The app's support for achieving goals and recognizing accomplishments can increase the user's self-esteem and confidence in their abilities. The motivation and encouragement provided by the app can give the user the boost they need to move forward and achieve their goals.
