[What is MyONE?]

MyONE is an app that acts as a virtual friend and assistant to the user. The app leverages artificial intelligence technology to provide support and help users achieve their life goals. Whether it's motivation, guidance, or just the need to talk to someone.

MyONE is designed to be a continuous source of encouragement and positivity in daily life. With advanced artificial intelligence technology, users can expect the app to provide personal and meaningful interactions that make it a true friend.

[Why MyONE?]

The MyONE app has a positive impact on the user's mental health in several ways. MyONE provides a friendly, personal support to the user, which aims to increase the user's sense of significance and the feeling of being understood. This can help reduce feelings of loneliness and generally improve well-being.

The app's support for achieving goals and recognizing accomplishments can increase the user's self-esteem and confidence in their abilities. The motivation and encouragement provided by the app can give the user the boost they need to move forward and achieve their goals.

[For whom is MyONE?]

MyONE is suitable for anyone, whether you're looking for answers to everyday questions, challenges, weighty matters, or just someone to talk to. It is user-friendly, so it is easy to use even for those with less technology knowledge.

[Pricing]

If and when you want all of our MyOne offerings for new customers, a free 7-day trial that doesn't bind you to anything yet. You can use MyOne for a monthly price of 10 euros or for a fixed period of 12 months/100 euros. Termination can be done at any time, and the service is available after the paid day after termination.
